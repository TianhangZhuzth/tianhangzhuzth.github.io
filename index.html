Tianhang Zhu

Tianhang Zhu is Head of Reinforcement Learning at 01.ai, leading online RLHF training, reward modeling, and reasoning research for Yi models. Previously he was a Senior Research Scientist on the Qwen LLM team at Alibaba DAMO, where he helped deploy Qwen-max and co-authored the Qwen and Qwen2 technical reports. He holds two master's degrees from the Georgia Institute of Technology and a Bachelor's in Computer Science and Actuarial Science from the University of Waterloo. His research interests include reinforcement learning from human feedback, large language model training, and multi-agent systems.
