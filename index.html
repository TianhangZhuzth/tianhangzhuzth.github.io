Tianhang Zhu

Tianhang Zhu is a Research Scientist on the Qwen LLM team at Alibaba DAMO, working on reinforcement learning from human feedback and large-scale language model training. He holds two master's degrees from the Georgia Institute of Technology and a Bachelor's in Computer Science and Actuarial Science from the University of Waterloo. His research interests include reinforcement learning, large language models, and multi-agent systems.
